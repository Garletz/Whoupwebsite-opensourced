How to Delete Your Account and Data

Step 1: Access Account Settings

Open the Whoup app and tap on your profile icon to access account settings.

Step 2: Navigate to Delete Account

Scroll down and tap on "Delete my account and all datas" in the settings menu.

Step 3: Confirm Deletion

Read the warning message about data deletion. This action will permanently delete:

  • Your profile information
  • All your photos and content
  • Your voting history
  • Your ranking data
  • All associated account data

Step 4: Final Confirmation

Enter your password and tap "Delete My Account" to confirm the deletion.

⚠️ Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently erased from our servers within 30 days.

Need Help?

If you're having trouble deleting your account, please contact our support team at support@whoup.site
