Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

WhoUp is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the safety of your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

2. Data We Collect

- Personal Information: Name, profile photo, and email address. - Usage Data: Votes, ranking statistics, user interactions, and session data. - Device Information: IP address, device type, and app usage analytics.

3. How We Use Your Data

- To provide and improve the services of WhoUp. - To personalize the user experience, including recommendations. - To ensure compliance with app policies and prevent misuse. - To analyze app performance and user engagement trends.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your data with third parties except for: - Service providers who assist in maintaining our platform (e.g., hosting, analytics). - Compliance with legal obligations or enforcement of app policies. - Aggregated, anonymized data for research or marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

- User data is encrypted and stored securely on our Firebase database. - Cloudflare and internal measures protect against breaches. - However, no system is completely immune to risks. Users acknowledge this and consent to data usage under these terms.

6. User Control

- Users can delete their accounts and associated data anytime via the app. - Settings allow users to review, update, or request deletion of their personal data. - Note: Some data may remain in backups for a limited time per security and compliance needs.

7. Cookies and Tracking

- WhoUp may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience. - These are used solely to track performance metrics and user preferences, not for invasive profiling.

8. Compliance

- WhoUp adheres to GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California) regulations to ensure user privacy. - Users have the right to request access, deletion, or transfer of their data under these laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Users will be informed of major changes via the app or email.
