Get Verified on Whoup

Step 1: Prepare Required Documents

  • Your Whoup account email address
  • A clear photo/scan of your ID card or passport
  • A screenshot of your social media profile (Instagram preferred)

Step 2: Send Verification Request

Send an email to security@whoup.site with:

  • Subject line: "Whoup Verification Request"
  • Your Whoup account email
  • Attached ID document
  • Screenshot of your social media profile
  • Your social media handle/username

Step 3: Monitor Your Social Media

Our team will contact you through direct message on your social media account to confirm your identity.

⚠️ Important Information

  • Verification process typically takes 3-5 business days
  • Make sure your social media account accepts direct messages
  • All documents are securely stored and handled according to our privacy policy
